Nutrition

Vitamin A
Fish liver oil, carrots, butter and milk
Deficiency Diseases
Xerophthalmia (hardening of cornea of eye) and night blindness
Vitamin B1
Yeast, milk, green vegetables and cereals
Deficiency Diseases
Beri beri (loss of appetite, retarded growth)
Vitamin B2
Milk, eggwhite, liver, kidney
Deficiency Diseases
Cheilosis, digestive disorders and burning sensation of the skin
Vitamin B6
Yeast, milk, egg yolk, cereals and grams
Deficiency Diseases
Convulsions
Vitamin B9
Fortified grains and cereals, asparagus, okra, spinach, turnip greens and broccoli
Deficiency Diseases
Fatigue, weakness, mouth sores and neurological issues
Vitamin B12
Meat, fish, egg and curd
Deficiency Diseases
Pernicious anaemia (RBC deficient in haemoglobin)
Vitamin C
Citrus fruits, amla and green leafy vegetables
Deficiency Diseases
Scurvy (bleeding gums)
Vitamin D
Exposure to sunlight, fish and egg yolk
Deficiency Diseases
Rickets (bone deformities in children) and osteomalacia (soft bones and joint pain in adults)
Vitamin E
Vegetable oils like wheat germ oil, sunflower oil
Deficiency Diseases
Increased fragility of RBCs and muscular weakness
Vitamin K
Green leafy vegetables
Deficiency Diseases
Increased blood clotting time
Calcium
Yogurt, cheese, milk, tofu, sardines, salmon and fortified juices
Deficiency Diseases
Rickets, osteoporosis, and osteopenia, as well as disruptions in the metabolic rate
Chloride
Salt (sodium chloride), soy sauce, processed foods
Deficiency Diseases
Hypochloremia
Chromium
Meat, poultry, fish, eggs, potatoes, some cereals, nuts and cheese
Deficiency Diseases
Deficiency of RBCs and iron metabolism
Iodine
Iodized salt, processed foods and seafood
Deficiency Diseases
Thyroid disease and, if serious, can cause permanent brain damage and intellectual disability in babies
Zinc
Red meat, poultry, oysters and some other seafood, fortified cereals, beans and nuts
Deficiency Diseases
Skin changes that look like eczema, cracks and a glazed appearance on the skin, nappy area and hands
